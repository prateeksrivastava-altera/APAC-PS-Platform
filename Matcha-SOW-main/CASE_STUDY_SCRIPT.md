# Matcha-SOW Case Study Script

---

## Accelerating Statement of Work creation with AI.

Sales and delivery teams identified a need to streamline how Statements of Work were created, managed, and analysed to ensure consistent quality, reduce turnaround time, and leverage historical pricing intelligence for more competitive proposals.

---

## Challenge

Our existing manual process for creating Statements of Work was inefficient and inconsistent. Sales teams spent hours searching through folders of historical documents, copying and pasting content from multiple sources, and manually formatting each SOW from scratch. With numerous clients, products, engagement types, and varying resource requirements, each SOW became a time-consuming exercise prone to inconsistency, formatting errors, and missed components. This created challenges for maintaining professional quality standards, meeting tight proposal deadlines, ensuring accurate pricing based on historical data, and capturing institutional knowledge about successful engagements. The lack of visibility into historical SOW data also meant teams couldn't effectively analyse pricing trends, resource allocation patterns, or identify their most valuable client relationships.

---

## Approach

We partnered with Claude Code AI to co-develop the Matcha-SOW application – a solution designed to centralise and automate end-to-end SOW creation and management. The focus was on reducing document creation time, ensuring consistent quality through template-based generation, and enabling data-driven pricing and resource decisions through comprehensive analytics.

---

## AI Integration

Leveraging Claude Code AI and the Matcha API platform within our development environment, the team enabled intelligent content generation, analysis, and knowledge management capabilities, allowing us to:

- **Automatically generate professional SOWs** in minutes rather than hours using AI-powered content creation
- **Leverage historical templates** to guide AI generation, ensuring consistency with proven successful formats
- **Contextualise content intelligently** based on client account, product, engagement type, and detailed project requirements
- **Build an organisational knowledge bank** of historical SOWs with associated pricing, resource allocation, and client data
- **Track comprehensive resource hours** across eight distinct roles (PM, IC, SA, SE, Trainer, Integration, APAC Testing, APAC R&D)
- **Analyse pricing intelligence** across multiple currencies (USD, AUD, SGD) with statistical insights
- **Visualise business trends** through real-time analytics dashboards showing top accounts, product performance, and 12-month SOW generation timelines
- **Enable role-based collaboration** with administrators managing master data while team members focus on SOW generation
- **Export in multiple formats** (PDF, DOCX, TXT) for immediate client delivery or further customisation

The system integrates account management, product catalogues, engagement type classification, and template management into a unified platform. Professional export formatting ensures deliverables meet enterprise standards every time.

- **Dramatic time savings** by reducing SOW creation from hours to minutes through AI-powered generation

## Use Case Examples

**Scenario 1: Rapid Response Proposal**
A sales team member receives an urgent RFP with a 48-hour turnaround requirement. Using Matcha-SOW, they select a similar historical template, input the client's specific requirements, and generate a professional SOW in 12 minutes. They review the AI-generated content, export to PDF for client delivery, and to DOCX for internal review – all within 30 minutes total. Proposal submitted with 24 hours to spare.

**Scenario 2: Data-Driven Pricing**
A delivery manager preparing a proposal reviews the dashboard analytics to understand pricing patterns for similar implementations. The system shows that comparable engagements for this product typically range from $450K-$650K USD, with an average of 2,800 resource hours across specific role types. Armed with this intelligence, they confidently price their proposal at $575K with appropriate resource allocation, winning the deal while maintaining healthy margins.

**Scenario 3: Knowledge Preservation**
A senior consultant who led successful implementations is leaving the company. Before departure, the team uploads their 15 most successful SOWs to the Knowledge Bank with complete pricing and resource data. This institutional knowledge remains accessible to the entire team, ensuring future proposals can leverage these proven approaches.
