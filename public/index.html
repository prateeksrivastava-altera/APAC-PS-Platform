Platform briefing

This hub is the front door for APAC Professional Services applications. It is intended to consolidate troubleshooting, enablement, script generation, and other delivery accelerators behind one consistent interface.

Current live capability

The hub currently exposes two live applications: Troubleshooting Platform for Matcha-guided issue triage and Training Platform for APAC AI onboarding, learning paths, and mentor-led enablement.

How the platform is structured

Each application appears as a selectable item in the left navigation. Selecting an app loads its dedicated experience into the main panel so users stay within one shared APAC PS workspace.

Planned expansion

Additional accelerators such as Test Script Generator remain visible in the navigation to show roadmap intent, but they stay disabled until their workflows are implemented.
